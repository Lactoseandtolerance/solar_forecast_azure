About Our Forecasting

Our solar forecasting system uses machine learning models trained on historical weather and solar production data. The predictions account for:

  • Cloud cover and weather conditions
  • Temperature variations
  • Seasonal patterns
  • Time of day
  • Solar panel specifications at each location

The model provides forecasts with an accuracy typically within ±10% of actual production values.
