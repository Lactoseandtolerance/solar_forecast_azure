About the Project

This solar energy forecasting system uses machine learning to predict solar power generation based on weather conditions and historical data. By leveraging cloud infrastructure, we provide reliable forecasts that help optimize energy distribution and reduce operational costs.

Our models take into account various factors including cloud cover, temperature, time of day, and seasonal patterns to deliver accurate predictions within ±10% error margin.

How It Works

  1. Weather data is collected in real-time from multiple sources
  2. Historical solar production data is analyzed for patterns
  3. Machine learning models process the data to generate forecasts
  4. Results are delivered through an intuitive dashboard
  5. Forecasts are continuously evaluated and models improved
